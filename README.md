# Brazil Agriculture Digital Transformation

This project is a research analysis of how advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, big data analytics, the Internet of Things (IoT), and agentic AI are reshaping Brazil’s agricultural sector.

The paper explores the evolution of Brazilian agriculture from mechanization to precision agriculture and now to intelligent digital ecosystems. It also examines the broader impact of these technologies on sustainability, cybersecurity, policy, workforce development, and global food systems.

A major focus of this research is how Brazil’s agricultural transformation connects to larger supply chains and trade networks, including links to Houston as a major U.S. logistics hub.

## Key Topics
- Artificial Intelligence in agriculture
- IoT and smart sensing technologies
- Big data and digital ecosystems
- Sustainability and food security
- Cybersecurity risks in digitized agriculture
- Brazil’s global trade and supply chain influence
